  [1X2.1 [33X[0;0YIntroduction[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YLet  [23Xk[123X  be  a  field  and  [23XI[123X  be a totally ordered set. We denote the matrix
  category     of     [23Xk[123X     by     [23Xk\mathrm{-vec}[123X     (see     the     package
  [23X\texttt{LinearAlgebraForCAP}[123X).  The  semisimple category [23X\bigoplus_{i \in I}
  k\mathrm{-vec}[123X  associated  to [23Xk[123X and [23XI[123X is defined as the full subcategory of
  the  product  category  [23X\prod_{i  \in  I}  k\mathrm{-vec}[123X generated by those
  [23XI[123X-indexed  tuples  having only finitely many non-zero entries. By [23X\chi^i[123X, we
  denote  the object which is [23X1[123X at entry [23Xi[123X and [23X0[123X otherwise. Thus, an arbitrary
  object in [23X\bigoplus_{i \in I} k\mathrm{-vec}[123X can be written as [23X\oplus_{i \in
  I}a_i  \chi^i[123X  for non-negative numbers [23Xa_i[123X for which only finitely many are
  non-zero.[133X
